### 1. Sell Aggregated, Anonymized Insights (B2B)

**What's allowed:**
- Trend data from 100+ users combined
- "35% of users prefer earth tones" (no individual data)
- "Average wardrobe has 47 items" (statistical)

- Must be aggregated (minimum 100 users typically)

**Why this is legal:**

- Aggregated statistical data
- Legitimate business purpose
